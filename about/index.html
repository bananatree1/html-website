ABOUT BANANA TREE CONSULTING

Founder Liz Solms has lived and worked in the Caribbean for over a decade. She has completed successful projects at private coastal villas, high-end hotels, coffee farms, and fruit plantations. Liz has worked for NGO’s that focus on sustainable agriculture, establishing one of the first organic farming cooperatives in the West Indies, in addition to establishing the Caribbean’s first farm-to-table dinner series.
